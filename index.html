Qatar 2022

Definitely, the peak moment of Leo Messi's entire career.

Qatar 2022 was the first World Cup held in the Middle East and during winter to avoid extreme heat. With cutting-edge stadiums, all located within a 50km radius, and advanced cooling technologies, it featured thrilling matches and dramatic comebacks, culminating in Lionel Messi's emotional farewell with Argentina's victory.

Russia 2018

Francia toca la gloria otra vez ante la Croacia de Modric.

Russia 2018 introduced the VAR system and was praised for its organization and hospitality. France's young squad, led by Mbappé and Griezmann, dominated the tournament. Croatia reached its first-ever final, showcasing remarkable resilience. It also recorded the most own goals in tournament history (12).

Brazil 2014

Germany wins for the Fourth Time, historic goal record by Klose and unforgettable performances.

Brazil 2014 offered electrifying matches and stunning upsets, most notably Germany’s 7-1 demolition of Brazil in the semi-finals. Germany became the first European nation to win a World Cup on South American soil. It was also the first tournament using goal-line technology to confirm goals.
